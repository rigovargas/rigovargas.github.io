Thanos

Infinity Stones === Rule of Galaxy & Unlimited Power

Thanos' ultimate goal is to seek and obtain all Infinity Stones so he can make himself omnipotent, and erase half the living things in the universe.


Restore Balance

Leading to believe the universe was unbalanced in terms of life and death and that Thanos was needed to restore that balance. Seeking the Infinity Stones, with a snap of a finger, Thanos was able to wipe out half of the universes' population of living beings and had enough power to make him an omnipotent being. http://marvel.com/universe/Thanos#ixzz5EVnLzO5G

Powers

Superhuman phsyiology, strength, endurance, reflexes and agility. Invulnerable skin against heat, cold, electricity, radiation, toxins, aging, and disease. Can survive without food or water. Invulnerable mind against psychic attack and can project a psionic blast of energy and plasma/cosmic energy from his eyes and hands.
